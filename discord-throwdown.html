Discord Community Throwdown

Summer 2018

The Yellowberri threw a video game tournament between Discord servers. 8 Overwatch teams threw down, with match commentary by up and coming casters, featuring stream overlays from yours truly.

This is also when a few YB members began community managing our YB Gaming Discord server, growing it into an awesome place and eventually meeting internet friends in real life!

Some neat things

  • Stream overlay scorebard, custom colors, animations
  • Animated webm video transitions
  • Hosted backend Node process sending websockets to local OBS
  • Camera switching via office NDI
  • Discord hype meter to displaying avg # of chat mentions in stream overlays
